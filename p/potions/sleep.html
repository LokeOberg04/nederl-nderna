Ingredients

  • Dream Blossom Essence: Extracted from the rare Dream Blossom, a flower that blooms in the ethereal realm during the midnight hours. Its essence is believed to hold the power to induce vivid and peaceful dreams.
  • Lunar Dewdrops: Collected from leaves touched by the silvery light of a full moon, these dewdrops are thought to carry the calming influence of moonlight, contributing to the elixir's sleep-inducing properties.
  • Whispering Willow Bark: Harvested from the branches of the Whispering Willow, a mystical tree known for its soothing presence. The bark is believed to carry the tree's calming whispers, easing troubled minds.
  • Starlight Crystals: Shimmering crystals formed during a celestial event, these crystals are said to capture the essence of starlight, infusing the elixir with a gentle, otherworldly glow.

Creation Process

  • Combine Dream Blossom Essence, Lunar Dewdrops, and Whispering Willow Bark in a crystal cauldron, stirring gently under the moon's glow.
  • Allow the mixture to absorb the energy of starlight, adding Starlight Crystals one by one.
  • Infuse the concoction with intentions of restful slumber and peaceful dreams, whispering ancient incantations during the brewing process.
